Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use IOTA Wallet and our services.

Download PDF Contact Privacy Team

Last Updated: March 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how IOTA Foundation ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use IOTA Wallet.

Privacy Overview

At IOTA Foundation, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our IOTA Wallet application and related services.

Key Privacy Principles

  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect information that is necessary for the operation and improvement of our services.
  • User Control: You have control over your personal information and can manage your privacy settings at any time.
  • Transparency: We are transparent about what information we collect and how we use it.
  • Security: We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • IOTA Wallet desktop and mobile applications
  • Our official website and web services
  • Customer support interactions
  • Community platforms we operate

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you create an account or profile, we may collect your email address, username, and other contact details.
  • Support Communications: When you contact our support team, we collect the information you provide in your communications.
  • Feedback and Surveys: Information you provide when participating in surveys or providing feedback about our services.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use IOTA Wallet, we may automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the wallet, including features accessed and time spent.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, including operating system, version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Network Information: IP address, network connection type, and general location information.
  • Error Reports: Crash reports and error logs to help us improve the application.

Blockchain Data

IOTA Wallet interacts with the IOTA Tangle, a public distributed ledger. Please note:

  • Transaction data on the Tangle is public and immutable
  • We do not control or have the ability to delete blockchain data
  • Your wallet addresses and transaction history are visible on the public ledger

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve IOTA Wallet functionality and features.
  • User Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and resolve technical issues.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security breaches, and other harmful activities.
  • Communication: To send you important updates, security alerts, and service announcements.

Analytics and Improvement

We may use aggregated and anonymized data to:

  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Improve user experience and interface design
  • Develop new features and services
  • Monitor and improve application performance

Legal Compliance

We may process your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Respond to lawful requests from authorities
  • Protect our rights, property, and safety
  • Enforce our terms of service

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our services, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of our users and the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate notice to users.
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes not covered by this policy.

Third-Party Services

IOTA Wallet may integrate with third-party services such as:

  • Hardware wallet providers
  • Analytics services (with anonymized data)
  • Cloud storage providers for backup services
  • Customer support platforms

These integrations are governed by the respective third parties' privacy policies.

International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements.
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your personal information.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please:

  • Contact our privacy team at privacy@iota.org
  • Use the privacy settings within the IOTA Wallet application
  • Submit a request through our support portal

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

Children's Privacy

IOTA Wallet is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Security Measures

How we protect your information and maintain security

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols including AES-256 and TLS 1.3.

Access Controls

Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms ensure only authorized personnel can access your information.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits and assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact us.
