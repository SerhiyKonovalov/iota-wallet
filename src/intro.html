What is IOTA Wallet?

IOTA is a distributed ledger technology that goes beyond blockchain, designed specifically for the Internet of Things (IoT) ecosystem. Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies, IOTA uses a unique architecture called Tangle, which enables feeless transactions and infinite scalability.
