Regulatory Information

Compliance and regulatory details for our operations

Non-Profit Foundation

IOTA Foundation is a non-profit organization established under German law. Our mission is to support the development and standardization of new distributed ledger technologies, including the IOTA Tangle.

Foundation Objectives

  • Research and development of distributed ledger technologies
  • Promotion of open-source software development
  • Education and awareness about blockchain and IoT technologies
  • Standardization efforts in the distributed ledger space
  • Supporting the IOTA ecosystem and community

Governance Structure

The Foundation operates under a transparent governance structure with:

  • Board of Directors providing strategic oversight
  • Advisory Board with industry experts
  • Regular financial audits and reporting
  • Community involvement in key decisions

Regulatory Compliance

IOTA Foundation maintains strict compliance with applicable laws and regulations across all jurisdictions where we operate.

European Union Compliance

  • GDPR: Full compliance with General Data Protection Regulation
  • MiCA: Monitoring and preparing for Markets in Crypto-Assets regulation
  • AML/CTF: Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorism Financing measures
  • eIDAS: Electronic identification and trust services compliance

German Law Compliance

  • BaFin: Coordination with German Federal Financial Supervisory Authority
  • KWG: Banking Act compliance where applicable
  • TMG: Telemedia Act compliance for digital services
  • BDSG: Federal Data Protection Act adherence

International Standards

  • ISO 27001 information security management
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance for service organizations
  • FATF recommendations for virtual assets
  • IEEE standards for distributed ledger technologies
